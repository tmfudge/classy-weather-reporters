Using Machine Learning to rapidly test pictures for sky conditions

Training picture: Cloudy

While it is easy for a human to determine the sky condition via looking there are advantages to using an automated process to do it as well. Some reasons could be the ability to remotely gather weather conditions for scientific studies, predict other driver behavior and road conditions in autonomous vehicles, or help the hard of seeing and blind with knowing their envirionment better. To achieve this a tensor flow algorithm was trained on data with pictures such as the one above to make class predictions between sunny, cloudy, and rainy. this is a limited tool as it stands but could be better optimized with more thurough training. For examples of it in action there are trafic cames from across the world that you can explore on this website.
